# Step-by-Step Guide: How to Use the Web-Based Sales Monitoring System  
**Role: Administrator**  

This guide walks you through the main features of the **Web-Based Sales Monitoring System for Loyal‚Äôs Printing Services** from the administrator‚Äôs perspective. Each section corresponds to a button in the sidebar menu and describes the content and actions available.

---

## 1. **Dashboard**
**Purpose:** Get an overview of business performance at a glance.

### Steps:
1. After logging in, you will land on the **Dashboard**.
2. View the following key information:
   - **Sales figures per branch** (total sales, recent transactions)
   - **Monthly transaction count** broken down by service type (Printing, Photo, Lamination, Photocopy, etc.)
   - **Quick access cards** for:
     - Branch Management
     - Sales Data
     - Messages
     - Inventory
3. Use the dashboard to monitor real-time performance and identify trends.

---

## 2. **Branches**
**Purpose:** Manage all branches of Loyal‚Äôs Printing Services.

### Steps:
1. Click **Branches** in the sidebar.
2. View the list of existing branches with:
   - User ID
   - Branch Name
   - Location
   - Email
3. To **add a new branch**:
   - Click the **‚ÄúAdd Branch‚Äù** button.
   - Fill in the required details (Branch Name, Location, Email).
   - Save the entry.
4. To edit or delete a branch, use the action buttons (edit/delete) next to each branch entry.

---

## 3. **Sales**
**Purpose:** Generate and view sales reports.

### Steps:
1. Click **Sales** in the sidebar.
2. Select a **branch** from the dropdown (or choose ‚ÄúAll Branches‚Äù).
3. Choose the **report type**:
   - Daily
   - Monthly
   - Overall (Yearly)
4. You can also **select a specific date** using the date picker.
5. Click **Generate Report**.
6. View the report table containing:
   - Invoice ID
   - Customer Name
   - Quantity
   - Amount
   - Time & Date
7. To download the report:
   - Click **‚ÄúDownload PDF‚Äù**.
   - The report will be saved in PDF format.

---

## 4. **Messages**
**Purpose:** Communicate with staff across branches.

### Steps:
1. Click **Messages** in the sidebar.
2. View the message interface with:
   - **Inbox** (received messages)
   - **Compose** section
3. To **send a message**:
   - Type your message in the text box.
   - Click the **Send** button.
4. To view previous conversations, scroll through the message history.
5. Messages are real-time and help coordinate tasks, alerts, and announcements.

---

## 5. **Inventory**
**Purpose:** Monitor and manage stock levels across all branches.

### Steps:
1. Click **Inventory** in the sidebar.
2. View the **stock table** showing:
   - Item Name (Laminating Plastic, Long Bond Paper, Short Bond Paper, etc.)
   - Quantity per branch
   - Total available stock
3. Use the **search bar** to find specific items or branches.
4. To **add stock**:
   - Click the **‚ÄúAdd Stocks‚Äù** button.
   - A modal will appear with input fields for each item.
   - Enter the quantities to add.
   - Click **‚ÄúSave Stocks‚Äù**.
5. The system updates inventory in real time as sales are recorded.

---

## 6. **Staff Dashboard Access (View-Only)**
**Purpose:** Review staff activities and transactions.

### Steps:
1. The admin can view the **Staff Dashboard** but cannot perform staff-level transactions.
2. From the admin view, you can see:
   - **Today‚Äôs Total Stock Deduction**
   - **Today‚Äôs Transactions** (File, Type, Quantity, Total, Branch, Time)
3. If no transactions occurred, ‚ÄúNo Transaction Found Today‚Äù is displayed.

---

## 7. **User Management**
**Purpose:** Manage user accounts and permissions.

### Steps:
1. Navigate to **User Management** (if available in your sidebar).
2. View list of users (Staff, Admin).
3. To **add a user**:
   - Click **‚ÄúAdd User‚Äù**.
   - Enter details (Name, Email, Role, Branch).
   - Set login credentials.
4. To **edit or deactivate** a user, use the action buttons.

---

## 8. **Reports & Analytics**
**Purpose:** Access advanced reports and performance insights.

### Steps:
1. Click **Reports & Analytics** (if available).
2. Select report type:
   - Branch Performance Comparison
   - Sales Trends Over Time
   - Top-Selling Services
3. Customize date range and branch filter.
4. View charts/graphs and download data.

---

## 9. **System Settings**
**Purpose:** Configure system preferences and notifications.

### Steps:
1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar.
2. Adjust:
   - **Notification preferences** (low stock alerts, sales reports)
   - **Printing preferences** (receipt format, default paper type)
   - **System maintenance** (backup schedules, user session limits)

---

## 10. **Logout**
**Purpose:** Securely exit the system.

### Steps:
1. Click your **profile icon** or **Logout** in the sidebar.
2. Confirm logout.
3. You will be redirected to the login screen.

---

## üìå **Quick Tips for Administrators:**
- Use the **Dashboard** daily for a snapshot of business health.
- Generate **Sales Reports** at the end of each day/week for record-keeping.
- Check **Inventory** regularly to avoid stockouts.
- Use the **Chatbox** for urgent communication with branch staff.
- Regularly **backup data** via Settings if the feature is available.

---

This guide covers all major functions available to an administrator in the **Web-Based Sales Monitoring System**. For further assistance, refer to the user manual or contact the development team.



# Step-by-Step Guide: How to Use the Web-Based Sales Monitoring System  
**Role: Staff**  

This guide walks you through the main features of the **Web-Based Sales Monitoring System for Loyal‚Äôs Printing Services** from the staff‚Äôs perspective. Each section corresponds to a button in the sidebar menu and describes the content and actions available.

---

## 1. **Dashboard / Home**
**Purpose:** View daily transactions and upload files for printing.

### Steps:
1. After logging in, you will land on the **Staff Dashboard**.
2. View **Today‚Äôs Transactions** table showing:
   - File name
   - Service type
   - Quantity
   - Total amount
   - Time
3. If no transactions today, ‚ÄúNo Transaction Found Today‚Äù is displayed.
4. To **upload files** for printing:
   - Click **‚ÄúUpload Files‚Äù** section.
   - Drag & drop files or click to browse.
   - Supported formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX, JPG, PNG.
   - Max file size: 10 MB.
   - Enter **Customer‚Äôs Full Name**.
   - Click **Submit**.
5. To report issues:
   - Enter details in **‚ÄúPaper Jam‚Äù** field if applicable.
   - Select **Coupon Bond Type** from dropdown.
   - Click **Save**.

---

## 2. **Upload Files / Print Orders**
**Purpose:** Upload customer files and select print options.

### Steps:
1. Click **Upload Files** in the sidebar (if separate from Dashboard).
2. Upload files as described in Step 1.
3. After upload, you‚Äôll see:
   - Batch details (# of files, pages, size, upload date).
4. Select **Print Service** from dropdown:
   - Xerox Long/Short/A4 Bond Paper
   - Printing Long/Short/A4 Bond Paper
   - Photo Print
   - Lamination
   - Book Binding (Long/Short/A4)
   - Tarpaulin
5. For printing options (e.g., Printing Long Bond Paper):
   - Choose: Plain (‚Ç±3), Apparent (‚Ç±5), Half (‚Ç±8), Full Colored (‚Ç±10).
6. Enter **Number of Copies**.
7. System calculates **Total Cost** automatically.
8. Click **Preview** to check file or **Confirm Order** to proceed.

---

## 3. **Pricing**
**Purpose:** View and confirm pricing options for services.

### Steps:
1. Click **Pricing** in the sidebar.
2. View all available services with prices:
   - Xerox, Printing, Photo, Lamination, Book Binding, Tarpaulin.
3. Pricing is displayed per page/copy.
4. Use this as reference when entering customer orders.

---

## 4. **Inventory / Stocks**
**Purpose:** View and update stock levels for your branch.

### Steps:
1. Click **Inventory** or **Stocks** in the sidebar.
2. View **Today‚Äôs Total Stock Deduction**:
   - Shows pieces deducted per item (Laminating Plastic, Bond Papers, Photo Paper).
3. View **Today‚Äôs Transactions** (same as Dashboard).
4. To **add stocks**:
   - Click **‚ÄúAdd Stocks‚Äù** button.
   - A modal opens with input fields for each item.
   - Enter quantities to add.
   - Click **‚ÄúSave Stocks‚Äù**.
5. Stock levels update automatically after sales.

---

## 5. **Messages / Chatbox**
**Purpose:** Communicate with admin and other branches.

### Steps:
1. Click **Messages** in the sidebar.
2. View message history with admin/other staff.
3. To send a message:
   - Type in the text box at the bottom.
   - Click **Send** button.
4. Use for:
   - Reporting low stock
   - Asking questions
   - Receiving announcements from admin

---

## 6. **Sales Entry (if separate module)**
**Purpose:** Manually enter sales if not auto-recorded via file upload.

### Steps:
1. Click **Sales Entry** (if available).
2. Fill in:
   - Customer Name
   - Service Type (dropdown)
   - Quantity
   - Paper Type/Options
   - Amount
3. Click **Save Transaction**.
4. Transaction appears in Today‚Äôs Transactions.

---

## 7. **View Reports (Staff-Level)**
**Purpose:** View sales reports for your branch only.

### Steps:
1. Click **Reports** in the sidebar.
2. Select report type:
   - Daily Sales
   - Monthly Sales
3. Reports show:
   - Invoice ID
   - Customer
   - Service
   - Quantity
   - Amount
   - Date/Time
4. Staff can view but **cannot generate PDF** (admin-only).

---

## 8. **Profile / Account**
**Purpose:** View and update your staff profile.

### Steps:
1. Click your **Profile** icon/name in top-right.
2. View:
   - Name
   - Email
   - Branch
   - Role
3. To change password:
   - Click **Change Password**.
   - Enter current and new password.
   - Save.

---

## 9. **Logout**
**Purpose:** Securely exit the system.

### Steps:
1. Click your **Profile icon**.
2. Select **Logout**.
3. Confirm if prompted.
4. Redirected to login page.

---
